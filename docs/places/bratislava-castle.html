Bratislava Castle

Landmark

Iconic hilltop castle with panoramic views of the Danube.

  • Zone: Castle Hill
  • Recommended time: 1.5–2 hours

Curiosities

  • The castle has been rebuilt multiple times, most recently after a 19th-century fire.
  • On clear days, you can see Austria and Hungary from the terrace.
